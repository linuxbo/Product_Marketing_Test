Viewing the Reports Dashboards

The Dashboards show you a variety of data about your store, including data about orders, refunds, site traffic, payments, and other activity on your store.

When viewing the Dashboards, the menu along the top provides access to the many different types of reports available in the Dashboards. For example, the "Products" menu will show you a Dashboard that has many different data and graphs about the products on your store, including the top selling products and the products that get returned the most.

To view the Dashboards:

Role Needed: Reporting ManagerA user role that provides access to online reports. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information.
  • On the Reports menu, click Dashboards. A new window opens, showing the first set of Dashboards reports. Click the menu options along the top to view the different types of Dashboards reports.

Note: If you want to view the Dashboard for subscriptions, click the Subscriptions link in the upper-right corner of the page.



If you do not see the Subscriptions link as shown in the picture above, contact your Store Operations team.

Filtering and Viewing Data

Some Dashboards will require you to select how you want to filter or view the data for the report. This may include the time frame you are viewing, as well as the company, site, locale, or currency you want to view. Other Dashboards may just allow you to choose the metric you want to view, for example gross sales, net order, refunds, and so on.

To learn more about the specific data elements you see in the Dashboards, see Reports Dashboards Data Definitions.
