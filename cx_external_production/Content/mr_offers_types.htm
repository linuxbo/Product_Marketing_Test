Types of Offers You Can Create

There are several different types of offers you can create. Each type has specific options and settings that determine how the offer works and behaves on your store.

Note: When you are creating an offer, some of the types listed in this topic may not appear if your site has not been set up to use them. See Configuring Offer Types for more information about how to set up offer types for your store.

General Site or Product Discount:
  • Discount – Provides a general discount on specific products or all products on your site.
Relationship Between Multiple Products:
  • Up-sell – A bundled offer that presents the shopper with a different product (usually one of higher quality or cost) when a specific product is added to the shopping cart.
  • Bundle – A bundled offer that presents a discounted product when another product is added to the shopping cart.
  • Buy M, Get N – A bundled offer that presents a free or discounted product when another product is purchased.
  • All Products Bundle – A bundled offer that presents a free or discounted product when any product is added to the shopping cart.
  • Shopper-defined Bundle – Shoppers are presented with an interstitial page where they can choose from listed products to create their own bundle, or a discount can appear in the shopping cart if a tiered discount based on quantity exists.
  • Cross-sell – A bundled offer that presents the shopper with a product when another product is purchased.
Shipping Cost Discount:

Discount Shipping on Individual Product:

  • Shipping Individual Product – Provides a shipping discount on particular products or products in a specific category.
  • Shipping Individual Product + Shipping Method – Provides a shipping discount when a specific product or product in a specific category is purchased and a particular shipping method is used.
  • Shipping Individual Product + Shipping Destination – Provides a shipping discount when a specific product or product in a specific category is purchased and the order is being shipped to a specific destination.
  • Shipping Individual Product + Shipping Method + Shipping Destination – Provides a shipping discount when a specific product or product in a specific category is purchased and the order is being sent to a specific shipping destination, and a specific shipping method is used.

Discount shipping on order when a specific product is purchased:

  • Shipping Product Present – Provides a shipping discount when a specific product (or products) or product in a specific category is purchased. The shipping discount is applied to the entire order’s shipping costs.
  • Shipping Product Present + Destination + Shipping Method – Provides a shipping discount when the order contains a specific product or a product in a specific category, and the order is being sent to a specific shipping destination, and a specific shipping method is used. The discount is applied to the whole order.

Discount shipping on order regardless of products purchased:

  • Shipping Destination – Provides a shipping discount for specific destinations. Orders that are not being shipped to the destination set up in the offer will not be able to take advantage of the discount.
  • Shipping Method – Provides a shipping discount (amount off or percentage of cost) when a specific shipping method is used.
  • Shipping Order Value – Provides a discount when the order purchase amount meets or exceeds a specific amount.
  • Shipping Order Value + Destination – Provides a discount when the order purchase amount meets or exceeds a specific amount and is being shipped to a specific destination.
  • Shipping Order Value + Shipping Method – Provides a shipping discount when the order purchase amount meets or exceeds a specific value and a particular shipping method is used.
  • Shipping Product Quantity – Provides a shipping discount when the product quantity in the shopping cart meets or exceeds a certain value. If desired, you can set the maximum number of products allowed for the offer.
  • Shipping Product Quantity + Destination + Shipping Method – Provides a shipping discount when the product quantity falls within a specific range, and the order is being sent to a specific shipping destination, and a specific shipping method is used.
