Business Navigator: Sales by Sites

The Sales by Sites report shows sales that happened on all the sites that sell your products in the channel.

Fields in This Report

The following lists the fields that are contained in the Sales by Sites report.

  • DRHQ_REPORTING_DATE
  • Site ID
  • Locale
  • Version ID
  • Product ID
  • Product Name
  • Report Currency
  • Gross Sales
  • Gross Units
  • Return Sales
  • Return Units
  • Net Sales
  • Net Units
  • Month Year
