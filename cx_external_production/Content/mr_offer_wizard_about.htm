About the Create Offer Wizard

Offers are created using the Offer Wizard, which guides you through each step of the creation process. Each step of the wizard deals with one aspect of the offer setup, such as what products are included in the offer and how those products are priced or discounted. Not every step will be needed for every offer and depending on the type of offer you are creating you may not need to perform the third step where you select products for the offer, and you may have to choose a point of promotion (POP) for the offer, set up coupon codes for the offer, or define discount pricing tiers. You can always tell which step you are by viewing the "beads on a string" at the top of the page.

Summary of Steps

A summary of the steps in the Offer Wizard, as well as what you do in each step is given below. Please note that some steps may not be needed, depending on selections you make in previous steps.

  1. Step 1: Type: The first step of the Wizard is where you choose the offer type you want to create. It is also where you enter some basic information for the offer. creating an offer is to define the Offer Type.
    • Enter general information such as offer name and description.
    • You can optionally define a start and/or end date for the offer.
    • Choose the type of offer you want to create.
    • Choose how the offer will be triggered.
  2. Step 2: Behavior: The second step is where you define the Offer Behavior.
    • Choose the type of discount for the offer (All Products or Individual Products).
    • If the offer will be triggered by Coupon Codes, set up the coupon codes for the offer including usage limits for codes.
    • Specify any usage limits for the offer on your site.
    • Specify the locales on which the offer is supported, or specify that the offer is available on all locales.
    • If needed, you can select a POP for the offer.
  3. Step 3: Products and Pricing or Step 3: Pricing (Depending on the offer type): The third step is where you choose the Products and/or define Pricing for the offer. Products are only chosen if the offer has a Discount Type of Individual Products. Once you select products you can enter pricing for products in the offer.
  4. Search for the products you want to associate with the offer you search within a catalog for products by name, ID, or SKU.
  5. Choose the type of discount, including whether to discount by value (such as $25 off) or a percentage (such as 25% off).
  6. If the offer is to be a shipping discount offer you can set that discount pricing as well.
  7. If you have other locales set up for the offer you can set up the pricing for those locales and currencies.
  8. If needed, you can also set up discount tiers for volume discount offers.
  9. Step 4 or 5: Display Content: The fourth or fifth step (depending on the offer type) is to enter the Display Content for the offer on supported locales. Display content includes offer images, descriptions, header and footer text, and so on. The images or content you set up are only used for the offer and do not affect other images and content you have set up for products in your catalog.
  10. The final step is to review the Offer Summary and make any changes (if needed). When you save the offer you can choose to immediately deploy the offer on your site. If you do not deploy when the offer is created, you must deploy it at another time for it to appear on your site.
