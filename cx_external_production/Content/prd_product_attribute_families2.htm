About Custom and Common Product Attributes

An attribute is simply a characteristic of a product. Attributes are defined or selected when products are created, and are used when creating variations of a base product. Product attributes are the group of related attributes that define products and product variations.

There are two different types of product attributes: custom and common.

  • Custom Product Attributes are company– or site–specific attributes that are specific to the client's products. Custom product attributes are usually created by Digital River, and are only available to the company for which they are created. To learn how to create custom product attributes for a company or client, see Creating Custom Product Attributes.
  • Common Product Attributes are used by all Digital River clients. These attributes are created and managed by Digital River and cannot be changed. You can, however view common product attributes and the individual attributes that make them up. For information about common product attributes, including how to view them, see Viewing Common Product Attributes.

Note: To work with product attributes, you must have the role of Family Manager assigned.
