Business Navigator: Call Center Agent Reports

Call Center Agent reports show sales activity by customer service representatives. These reports are primarily used to track sales by personnel and allocate commissions on their activities.

Report Name Description
Agent Transaction Detail Detailed customer information grouped at the agent level.
Demand - Agent Transactional Detail Detailed customer information grouped at the agent level.
Sales By Agent By Country Aggregated sales grouped at the country/agent level.
Sales By Country Aggregated sales at the country level.
