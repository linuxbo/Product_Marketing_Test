Creating Estimates for Buyers

To support telesales, customer service or company representatives can create estimates on behalf of buyers. Buyers will be emailed a copy of the estimate with a link so they can complete the order or view more information about their estimate.

  1. Open the store for which the buyer wants to place an order or get an estimate. Make sure you select the right locale so the correct currency, shipping, fees, and export controls will be applied to the order.
  2. Add the products to the cart.
  3. If there is an offer or promotion, make sure the cart meets the requirements for the offer or make sure you enter and apply the coupon code in the shopping cart.
  4. Click the Request Estimate button.
  5. Complete the estimate request form on behalf of the buyer:
    • Make sure to enter the buyer's name and email address so that they will be able to view the estimate later and complete their order as needed.
    • Enter up to two additional email addresses where the estimate should be sent. This could be other buyers at the buying company or anyone else the buyer would like to notify of the estimate.
    • Enter as much of the company information as you can for the estimate.
    • If the buyer agrees to be contacted again about their order make sure you select the "Contact Me" check box in the estimate request form.
  6. Once you have submitted the request form, the buyer (and anyone else they chose to be sent the estimate) will be sent a copy of their estimate via email. You may want to contact the buyer again about their estimate and help them complete their order.
