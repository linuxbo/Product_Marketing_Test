About Configuring Devices

You can configure which themes are used when a shopper used a mobile phone or tablet computer to view your store. This allows you to create themes that designed with the different needs and limitations of each type of device.

 

 

 

 

Opening the Configure Devices Page

  1. On the Store Builder menu, select Storefront, then click Configure Devices. The Configure Devices page appears.
