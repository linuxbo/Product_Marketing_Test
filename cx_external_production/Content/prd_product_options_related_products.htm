Related Products

The Related Products option is used to assign related products to a specific product. When that product is viewed on your store or used with a marketing offer, the related products you set up may appear.

Related products are often used with products that have accessories that are required for use with the product. This can include products such as printers and printer cartridges, laptops and laptop batteries, and computer games with expansion packs.

Using the Related Product Setting

There are no specific attributes that are set for Related Products. When you use this setting with a product you simply add the products you want to appear as "related products" and the system does the rest.
