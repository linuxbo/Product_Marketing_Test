Choosing the Offer Type

The offer type determines how the offer behaves and what additional settings you can define for the offer. There are two main types of offers: offers that discount product cost or order total, and offers that discount shipping costs.

Note: Some of the offer types listed below may not appear when you create an offer if your site has not been set up to use them. See Configuring Offer Types for more information about how to set up offer types for your site.

Offers that Discount Shipping Costs (For Products or the Entire Order)

The following outlines scenarios where you want to provide a discount on the shipping costs for a product or order.

Note: The options you see below may not be available on your site if your offers are not configured to allow them. See Configuring Offer Types for more information.

Discount Shipping for Any Product

You want to provide free or discounted shipping when... Shipping Offer Type UI Example
Any product from your catalog or from a category in your catalog is purchased. Individual Products
Any product from your catalog or a category in your catalog is purchased and the order is being sent to a specific country. Individual Products + Shipping Destination
Any product from your catalog or a category in your catalog is purchased  and a specific shipping method is used. Individual Products + Shipping Method
Any product from your catalog or a category in your catalog is purchased and the order is being sent to a specific country and a specific shipping method is used. Individual Products + Shipping Method + Shipping Destination

Discount Shipping for Entire Order

You want to provide free or discounted shipping when... Shipping Offer Type UI Example
A specific product(s) is purchased. Shipping Product Present
A specific product is purchased and the order is being sent to a specific country and a specific shipping method is used. Shipping Product Present + Destination + Shipping Method

Discount Shipping for Specific Products

You want to provide free or discounted shipping when... Shipping Offer Type UI Example
A certain number of a specific product is purchased. Shipping Product Quantity
A certain number of a specific product is purchased and the order is being sent to a specific country and a specific shipping method is used. Shipping Product Quantity + Destination + Shipping Method
The order is being sent to a specific country. Shipping Destination
The total value of all products in the order meets or exceeds a specific amount. Shipping Order Value
The total value of all products in the order meets or exceeds a specific amount  and a specific shipping method is used. Shipping Order Value + Shipping Method
The total value of all products in the order meets or exceeds a specific amount  and the order is being sent to a specific country. Shipping Order Value + Destination
A certain shipping method (UPS, USPS, FexEx) is used. Shipping Method
