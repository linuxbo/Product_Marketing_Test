Product Options

When you create a product you choose which product options you want to apply or enable for the product. Some options will define the type of product (for example, a software product or a physical product) while other options will control where or when the product can be sold, how the product is priced and fulfilled, and so on.

Note: The fields, options, attributes, and settings you see when you create a product can be configured using the Product Template. This means that some settings may not appear when you are working with your products (if your company/site has set the Product Template to hide them in the interface). If you have questions about why you are not seeing a particular attribute or setting, contact your Store Operations team. To learn more about the Product Template, see About the Product Template.

Adding or Enabling Product Options

Adding or enabling a product option varies depending on whether you are creating the product or editing an existing one.

  • When you are creating a product, the Product Options step is where you enable options you want the product to have. Later in the wizard you define the specifics for these options.

  • When you are editing a product, the Product Editor shows you the settings you have enabled for a product and has a link where you can add or remove options as needed.
