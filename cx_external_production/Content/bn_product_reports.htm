Business Navigator: Product Reports

Product reports show information about sales and returns, highlighting specific product names and types of products.

Report Name Description
Product Close Ratio Order close rates by product.
Product Level Sales Aggregated sales grouped at the product level.
Product Level Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/product level.
Product Level Sales By Day By Country Aggregated sales groups at the day and product level, by country.
Product Level Sales By MFR Part # Aggregated sales grouped at the manufacturer part number.
Web v Phone Aggregated sales grouped at the order origin level.
