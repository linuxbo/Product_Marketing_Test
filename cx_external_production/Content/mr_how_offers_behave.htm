How Offers Behave on Your Store

There are many things that influence how an offer appears and behaves on your store. The settings you choose for your offer determines the type of discount or promotion the offer takes, how long the offer is available, how an offer is redeemed by shoppers, what locales the offer supports, how many times an offer can be redeemed, where the offer appears on your store, and so on.
