About Subscription Reports

There are several different reports (in two areas of the Command Console) you can run to get information specific to your subscription orders and customers. You can run a Business Navigator subscription report or use one of the Reports Dashboards to see information about your subscriptions.

Note: Don't forget that there are many other reports you can use to view general information about your orders, shoppers, and so on. This topic only focuses on those reports that show subscription-specific information.

Business Navigator Reports

Subscription reports show detailed information about orders of subscription products.

Report Name Description
Cancel Details Detailed report showing subscriptions that have been discontinued.
Cancel Summary Aggregated report showing subscriptions that have been discontinued.
Failed Payment Details Detailed report showing failed transactions with failed authorizations.
Failed Payment Summary Detailed report showing failed transactions grouped at the summary level.
Subscription Transaction Detail Detailed report showing customer information.
Subscription Transaction Summary Aggregated report grouped at subscription type level.
