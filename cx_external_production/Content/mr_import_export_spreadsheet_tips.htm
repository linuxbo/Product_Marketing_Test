Tips for Using the Spreadsheets

The following outlines some tips for using the spreadsheets in the Import and Export tool:

  • To have an offer automatically deploy when a spreadsheet is imported, change the Status in the spreadsheet to "deployed."
  • Prepare any images, text, or translations before you begin working in the spreadsheets.
  • Do not enter anything in the "Offer ID" column. This is generated by the system.
  • Cells marked with an asterisk (*) are required and must be completed or the spreadsheet will not be imported.
  • When entering more than one value in a cell, use a semicolon (;) to separate the values. For example, to enter multiple Product IDs in the "Child Product IDs" column you would enter the following: 1087678; 1938439; 2738490.
  • Make sure the correct locale tab is selected at the bottom of the spreadsheet. The tabs at the bottom of the spreadsheet have two purposes. First, they separate the product content from the offer content and offer information. Second, they separate offer and product content for each locale. Offer content consists of how the offer is displayed, while product content holds the Product IDs with (optional) sales pitches and images for each.
