Site Trends Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Definitions

Understanding how the data in Site Trends is calculated and what it means is vital to using Site Trends. This topic defines the various key performance indicators (KPIs) you will see when viewing Site Trends.

Demand Sales

Demand sales refers to orders that have been placed but not yet fulfilled (downloaded or shipped).

Demand Sales
KPI Definition and Calculation
Sales

Definition: Total of all orders, after discounts. Does not include tax or shipping.
Orders

Definition: Number of orders placed.
Unique Visits

Definition: Number of distinct shoppers who visited your site.
Close Rate

Definition: Percentage of orders placed by the number of individual shoppers visiting your store.

Calculation: Orders / Unique Visitors
Shopping Basket Close Rate

Definition: Percentage of orders placed to the number of shopping baskets created by shoppers.

Calculation: Orders / Baskets Created
Revenue Per Visits

Definition: Total average order value, per individual shopper.

Calculation: Sales / Unique Visits
Average Order Value

Definition: Total average order value.

Calculation: Sales / Orders
Canceled Sales

Definition: Total of orders that have been canceled.
Backorder Sales

Definition: Total of orders on backorder.
Backorder Sales As a % Of

Definition: Percentage of demand sales that are on backorder.

Calculation: (Backorder Sales / Demand Sales) * 100
Canceled Sales As a % Of

Definition: Percentage of demand sales that were canceled.

Calculation: (Canceled Sales / Demand Sales) * 100

Fulfilled Sales

Fulfilled sales refers to orders that have been placed and fulfilled (downloaded or shipped).

Fulfilled Sales
KPI Definition and Calculation
Gross Sales

Definition: Total sales, after discounts. Does not include tax or shipping.
Gross Orders

Definition: Number of orders.
Net Sales

Definition: Total sales after returns are considered.

Calculation: Gross Sales - Returns
Net Orders

Definition: Number of orders after returns are considered.

Calculation: Gross Orders - Returns
Return Sales

Definition: Total returns. Does not include tax and shipping.
Return as a % of Gross

Definition: Percentage of gross sales that were returned.

Calculation: Return Sales / Gross Sales
Return Orders

Definition: Number of orders returned.
Average Order Value

Definition: Total average value for all orders.

Calculation: Gross Sales / Orders
Average Selling Price

Definition: Total average price for all products sold.

Calculation: Gross Sales / Gross Units
