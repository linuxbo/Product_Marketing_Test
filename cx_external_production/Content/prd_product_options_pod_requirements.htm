Physical on Demand Disc and Packaging Requirements

The files used for your Physical on Demand (POD) disc contents, disc label, and disc case image must meet specific requirements, as outlined in this section.

Disc Label Image Specifications

The disc label image is printed on the top of the disc. The following outlines the requirements for the disc label image you upload:

  • The image must be a JPEG (.jpg) file.
  • The image should be saved at 300 DPI resolution format. Larger images will not fit on the CD properly.
  • The image should measure no more than 4.75 inches. It will be printed on the top half of the CD. The bottom half is reserved for shipping and Digital River related information. If you need more space on your disc, contact your Store Operations team.

Disc Case Image Specifications

The Disc Case Image is what is printed on the insert that is in the DVD case that holds the disc. Basic disc case inserts are included with all POD products. Binding, stapling, or other unique printing requirements can be reviewed and approved on a case-by-case basis. The following outlines the requirements for the disc case image you upload:

  • The file must be a PDF (.pdf) file.
  • The size of the artwork should be 7.25 x 10.75 inches.
  • The file must include crop marks.
  • The file should have at least a 1/4 inch bleed. The bleed area is not printed, it ensures that your design extends to the edge of the cover.
  • The file must not have text that is closer than 1/4 inches from the edge. This ensures that the text will not get cut off the final printed cover.

Disc Content Files Specifications

The Disc Content Files are what is actually burned on the physical disc(s). Products that require multiple discs require prior approval by Digital River. Contact your Store Operations team if you product will require a multi-disc format.

  • The total size of the files cannot exceed the size of a normal CD (700 MB) unless prior approval has been given by Digital River.
  • For software programs and other files that customers need to install, we recommend that you wrap the files with an installer or similar mechanism to help customers install the program.
