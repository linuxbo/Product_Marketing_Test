About Subscriptions

A subscription is a product that a shopper gets on a recurring basis, such as every week, month, or year. Subscription products are charged on a per-recurrence basis, so if the subscription is set to renew every year the shopper gets charged for (and may receive) that product every year.

The Subscription option is used to create a subscription product and the options you set for the subscription determine how you want your subscriptions to behave, both on the storefront and once shoppers purchase the subscription on your store.

  • How Subscriptions Work – Learn more about how subscriptions behave and what certain actions for a subscription mean and how they affect the subscription product:
    • Setting subscription options
    • Subscription renewals and renewal pricing
    • Subscription cancellations
    • Subscription upgrades and downgrades
    • Subscription increase or decrease quantity
  • Subscription Site Settings – There are several site-level settings that enable certain subscription options and determine some behavior for subscriptions on your store.
  • Creating a Subscription Product – Get an overview of how to create a simple subscription product.
  • Offer Subscription Controls – When you create an offer, there are options you can use to control whether the offer will be used with new subscription orders or renewals.
  • Subscription Landing Page – You can use the subscription landing page to provide your shoppers a one-stop place to view and manage their subscription(s).
    • Shopper Self-Service Options – There are many things a shopper can do to manage their own subscription, including update their billing information and change their shipping address.
  • Customer Service Save – This option lets you set up a special incentive for shoppers to not cancel their subscription when they call Customer Service.
  • Subscription Reports – See how your subscriptions are performing with one of many Business Navigator reports.
