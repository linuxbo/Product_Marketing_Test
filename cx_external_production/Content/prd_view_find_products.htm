Viewing and Finding Products

There are two main ways that you can view and find products when working inside the Command Console: the Products list page and the Quick Search feature.

Products List Page

On the Products list page you can view the products that have been created for your store. You can use the Search and Filters pane on the page to change what type of products are seen in the list. To open the Products list page, on the Catalog menu, select Products, and click Manage Products.

Sorting the Products List

You can sort the products on the Products list page by any of the attributes you see in the list (product ID, internal product name, site display name, SKU, and so on). To sort, simply click on the column header you want to sort by. Click the column header again to reverse the sort.

Tip: Did you know that most list pages allow you to sort the list by clicking on the column headers? In fact, most of our pages use similar patterns for how they are used and how they behave. This means you will spend less time learning how to use the Command Console and more time managing your store and growing your business!

By default, the Products list only shows those products that have been assigned to a catalog and at least one category. However, you can change which products are being show in the Products list page using the Search and Filters pane.

Searching and Filtering the List

You can use the Search and Filters pane that appears on the list page to search the list by any of the criteria you sees. You simply enter the criteria you want to find in the fields available and click Search. The page will refresh to show only those products that match the criteria you have entered.

The Search and Filters pane can also be used to view products by their category assignment status. Products that have not been assigned to a category are given the "unassigned" status and can only be seen in the Products list when you select this status in the Search and Filters area.

Quick Search

The Quick Search feature can be used to quickly search for a product using name or product ID. Simply choose how you want to search your catalog and enter the search criteria in the field. When you click Go your catalog will be searched for products matching the criteria you have entered. For more about using Quick Search to find products, see Searching for Products with Quick Search.
