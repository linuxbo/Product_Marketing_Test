Business Navigator: Summary Reports

Summary reports provide a high-level view of sales activity, including both sales and traffic information.

Report Name Description
Daily Dashboard Sales Aggregated report showing KPIs.
Day By Day Host Activity Aggregated report showing sales and traffic information.
Delivery Method Aggregated report grouped by delivery method.
Demand Close Ratio Aggregated report showing sales and visitor information.
EDSCD2GO Aggregated report grouped at the EDS/CDToGo level.
Free Shipping Aggregated report for sales where free shipping occurred.
Page Drop Off Aggregated traffic report.
Sales By Country By Payment Method Aggregated report grouped at the country/payment method.
Sales By Site Aggregated sales grouped at the site level.
Shipping Method Aggregated sales grouped by delivery method.
Top Customer By Revenue Aggregated sales grouped by customer.
