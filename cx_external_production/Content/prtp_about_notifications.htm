About Partner Plan Notifications

There are several notifications that are sent to you and your partners throughout the signup and purchase process.

Notifications Sent to Partners

The following lists the events which trigger an email being sent to a partner. The text of the specific email may differ, depending on the customizations done for your store. Keep in mind that your partner may receive other emails, such as those regarding their account (user name and password).

  • Partner signs up for access to a partner plan.
  • Partner is approved or declined for a partner plan.
  • Partner is activated or deactivated for a partner plan.

    Partners can only be deactivated once they have already been approved for a partner plan. Similarly they can only be activated if they were previously deactivated or their access to a partner plan was declined.

Notifications Sent to You

You will get a notification when a partner signs up for access to a partner plan.
