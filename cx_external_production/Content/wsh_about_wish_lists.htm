About Wish Lists

The wish lists feature allows shoppers to create wish lists of products they want to purchase on your store. Shoppers can create multiple wish lists and make them public or private, depending on whether they want other people to see their wish lists or share their wish lists on social media or through email.

Note: If you want to offer wish lists on your store, you must enable the wish list feature in site settings. See Configuring Site Settings: Wish List for information about how to enable the wish list feature.

After shoppers create a wish list, they can view or change the items in their wish list, purchase items from their wish list, change the quantity of items in their wish list, delete items from their wish list, or make their wish list public or private.

Who Can Use Wish Lists

The wish list feature is only available to shoppers that have created an account on your store (also known as "authenticated users") and only available for stores using the rivulet template. See Determining the Template Used by the Site for more about how to find out what template your store uses.

Public vs Private Wish Lists

When a shopper creates (or edits) a wish list, the shopper can choose to make the wish list public or private:

  • Public wish lists can be seen by other shoppers on the store. Shoppers do not have to be authenticated or have an account with the store to view wish lists created by other authenticated users on the store. Public wish lists can be found by searching for the wish list ID, or the name and/or email of the person who created the wish list.
  • Private wish lists can only be seen by the person who created the wish list.
