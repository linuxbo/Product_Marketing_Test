Approving Refunds, Returns, and Replacements

After a Customer Service Representative initiates a return, refund, or replacement, approval may be required for the return, refund, or replacement to be processed.

Note: To approve a refund, return, or replacement, you must have the Approver role. This role can only be set by Digital River. Please contact your Store Operations Team with questions.

To approve a refund, return, or replacement:

  1. First, locate the order with the refund, return, or replacement that requires approval (on the Customer Service menu, select Search, and click Search Orders).
  2. Once you have located the order that requires approval, click the Pending Approval link that appears as the Order Status.
  3. In the window that appears, indicate you approve this return/refund/replacement.
