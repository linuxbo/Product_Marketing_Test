Viewing Offer Performance

When you view the details of an offer you can see the demand sales, close rate, and unique visitors for that offer. This allows you to edit or retire offers that are not performing as well as you hoped. This can also help you determine which offers are your best performers so you can replicate that success in other offers.

The information you see when viewing an offer is the same as what you see when viewing the Merchandising Dashboard, but the Merchandising Dashboard will show you more information about how your offer is performing.

There are two ways to locate and open an offer:

    
  • Use Quick Search to find an offer by name or ID.

 — or —
  • Navigate to the Offers list page (Marketing > Offers and Programs > Manage Offers) and click on the offer you want to view.

Viewing Performance

Once you have an offer open you will see a Performance area showing the total demand sales resulting from the offer, the close rate for orders placed for the offer, and the number of unique visitors that were presented the offer. To learn more about how these are calculated, see Viewing the Merchandising Dashboard.

  • Click Settings to open the panel where you can define the Time Frame and Currency you want to see. Make sure you click Apply Settings to apply any changes you make to the time frame and currency options.
  • Click Merchandising Dashboard to see more information about an offer on the Merchandising dashboard.
