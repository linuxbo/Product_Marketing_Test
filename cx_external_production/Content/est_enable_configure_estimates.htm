Enabling and Configuring Estimates

To provide your shoppers the ability to receive an estimate for their order you must enable the request estimates feature and define how you want it to behave in your site settings.

  1. On the Administration menu, select Site and then click Configure Site Settings. The Configure Site Settings page appears.
  2. Click the Checkout tab.
  3. In the Request Estimate area, select Enabled.

  1. For the Order Limit option, choose whether you want to allow buyers to place multiple orders for a single estimate or limit orders to one per estimate.
  2. For the Set Estimate Expiration option, choose how long you want an estimate to be valid after it is created.
  3. For the Send Reminder Email option, choose whether you want a reminder email sent to buyers after they create their estimate (buyers are only sent one reminder email). Reminders may help increase sales for estimates. If you choose to have a reminder email sent, you will have to enter the number of days after estimate creation when you want the email sent to the buyer.
  4. For the Display "Contact Me" Check Box option, select whether you want a "contact me" check box to appear so buyers can indicate they consent to being contacted by a company representative about their estimate or order. If you choose to include the contact me check box you must enter the email address of the company representative that you want to contact buyers about their estimate (a copy of the estimate where the shopper selects "contact me" will also be sent to this email address).
  5. Click Save.
