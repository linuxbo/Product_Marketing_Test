About Offer Types

There are several different types of offers you can create and each type has specific options and settings that determine how the offer works and behaves on your store. This topic outlines what the specific types of offers can (and cannot) do.

Note: Some of the offer types listed below may not appear when you create an offer if your site has not been set up to use them. See Configuring Offer Types for more information about how to set up offer types for your site.

Shipping Offers

Shipping offers provide a discount on shipping costs when certain conditions are met.

Discount Shipping on Individual Product(s)

The following offers provide a shipping discount on a specific product (or products) that you add to the offer itself. Shipping offers only discount shipping for the products you add to the offer and will not discount shipping for any other products in the order.
