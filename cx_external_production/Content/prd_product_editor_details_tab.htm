About the Product Editor: Details Tab

The majority of the work you will do with a product will be done on the Details tab. From the tab you can view and select settings for the product. You can also preview, deploy, copy, revert changes, and retire the product.

The following outlines all the tasks you can perform for products on the Details tab. Keep in mind that some of these tasks are not done for every product, or may not be available while the product has the "Deployed" status.

  • Adding and Removing Variations
  • Adding and Removing Locales
  • Adding and Removing Settings
  • Setting Product Pricing
  • Configuring Digital Rights
  • Preview the Product – To preview the product as it will appear on your site, click Preview in the taskbar. A new window opens showing a test version of your site and the current product.
  • Deploy the Product – To deploy the latest version of the current product, click Deploy in the taskbar. If the Deploy button is greyed out/disabled it means that the product cannot currently deployed, most likely because the product have deployment errors that must be fixed first.
  • Copy the Product – To copy the current product, click Copy in the taskbar.
  • Revert Changes – To revert the last set of changes deployed for the product, click Revert Changes. See About Reverting Changes to Products for more information.
  • Retire the Product – To retire the product, click Retire in the taskbar.
