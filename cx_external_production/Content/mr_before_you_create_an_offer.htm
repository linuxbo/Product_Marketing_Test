Things to Consider Before Creating an Offer

Before you create an offer there are several things you should know about how you want the offer to behave in order for you to set up the offer successfully in the Offer Wizard.

The following outlines the types of things you should consider before you begin creating an offer. Knowing how you want the offer to behave will help you identify the options and settings you should use to define your offer.

  1. What type of offer do you want to create (what do you want the offer to do)?
    • Do you want to provide a simple discount on one or more products?
    • Do you want to entice shoppers to buy a more expensive version of a product?
    • Do you want to bundle two or more products together for a greater discount?
    • Do you want to create an offer for a free gift (or free shipping) with purchase?
    • Do you want an offer that applies to all products within a specific category?
    • Do you want an offer that applies to all but just a few of your products?
  2. How will shoppers redeem the offer, that is how will the offer be triggered?
    • Do you want the offer to be "always on" so that any shopper who satisfies the criteria for the offer can redeem it?
    • Do you want shoppers to click a link in an email or social media campaign?
    • Do you want shoppers to click a point of promotion (POP) on your store or enter a coupon code to redeem the offer?
  3. Do you want to restrict how many times the offer can redeemed?
    • Do you want to restrict how often individual shoppers can redeem the offer?
    • Do you want to restrict how many times the offer can be redeemed on your store by all shoppers?
  4. How the offer will be communicated or presented to shoppers?
    • Will a banner ad or other type of point of promotion (POP) be used (or is required) on your store to inform shoppers of the offer? Some offers must use a particular POP to appear on your store correctly. See About Offer Types for more information.
    • Will you promote the offer in another way such as with email or a social media campaign?
  5. What (if anything) will the offer discount?
    • Will the offer discount all products or just some that you choose?
    • Will the offer give a discount on the price of the product (either a value or percentage of list price) or on the shipping costs associated with the product?
  6. What locales will the offer support or not-support?
    • Do you want to restrict which locales can redeem the offer?
    • Do you want the offer display content to be localized for your supported locales?
