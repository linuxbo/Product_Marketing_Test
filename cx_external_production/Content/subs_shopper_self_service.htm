About Subscription Shopper Self-Service Options

Shoppers who purchase a subscription product can view and manage the details for their subscription using FindMyOrder.com. We call this "self-service" as the shopper is the one who manages different aspects of their subscription using a shopper-friendly support site.

The following is a list of what shoppers can do with their subscription using features on FindMyOrder.com, the subscription landing page, or by calling Customer Service.

  • Manually renew a subscription. When a shopper has to renew their subscription themselves (versus it automatically happening) we call that a manual renewal subscription.
    • See Subscription Renewals for more information about subscription renewals.
  • Stop the automatic renewal of their subscription. When a shopper stops the automatic renewal of their subscription they are essentially turning their auto-renew subscription into a manual renew subscription.
    • See Subscription Renewals for more about how subscriptions renew.
  • Increase or decrease the number of subscription product(s). Shoppers can increase or decrease the quantity of a subscription product.
    • See About Increasing or Decreasing Subscription Quantity for more information about increasing and decreasing quantity of a subscription.
  • Upgrade or downgrade subscription product(s). In order to upgrade or downgrade a subscription, you first must have set up the initial subscription product with upgrade and downgrade products.
    • See About Subscription Upgrades and Downgrades for more information about how upgrades and downgrades work.
    • See Subscription Upgrade and Downgrade Products for more information about setting up upgrade or downgrade products.
  • Change the shipping address for the subscription. The shipping address is only used when the subscription uses physical products.
  • Update or change credit card billing information. Shoppers can change the credit card associated with their subscription or just update the details on the existing credit card.
  • Cancel a subscription. Shoppers can cancel their subscription. When a shopper cancels, their access to the subscription immediately ends.
    • See Subscription Cancellations for more information about canceling subscriptions.
