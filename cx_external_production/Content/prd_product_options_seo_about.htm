About the Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Option

The Search Engine Optimization (SEO) setting is used to set up the text and keywords you want to be used when the product is searched for and viewed with a list of search results from a search engine such as Google™. The terms you enter for SEO may help raise the product in the search results listing, depending on the specific terms entered by the shopper for the search.
