Point of Promotion (POP) Types

There are four main types of POPs that vary depending on their usage. This section outlines these four main types. Keep in mind that POPs and offers are highly customizable so what you see here may not accurately reflect what you see on your store.

Site Merchandising POPs

Site Merchandising POPs are used to display offer content on your store’s home page. We recommend that you use the Featured Products offer type with Site Merchandising POPs, unless otherwise specified.

POP Name

Definition

SiteMerchandising_HomePageStoreSpecials

List or group of special offers that appears on your store’s home page.

SiteMerchandising_HomePageTopSellers

List or group of top selling products that appears on your store’s home page.

SiteMerchandising_HomePageFeaturedItems

List or group of one or more featured products that appears on your store’s home page.

Banner_HomePageLocal

Banner image that appears on your store’s home page. The banner can be clickable to open a product details or category page or initiate some other action on the store (requires Site Design work).

SiteMerchandising_ProductDetailPageRelatedItems

List or group of related items on a product details page.

SiteMerchandising_ShoppingCartRecommendedItems

List or group of recommended items seen in the shopping cart.

Interstitial POPs

Interstitial POPs are used to show an offer that appears in another window when a shopper adds a product to the shopping cart. The following outlines some general guidelines for using the interstitial POPs with your offers:

  • We recommend you include no more than three products in your interstitial. Our tests have found that the number of products can affect how many shoppers redeem the offer.
  • Use the "Semi-Tight" bundle policy for Cross-Sells to ensure that shoppers buy the Parent product to get the Child (Cross-Sell) product. See About Bundle Offers for more about how to determine what bundle policy your offer assumes.
  • The Child product(s) in your cross-sell offers should be less expensive than the initial (Parent product) the shopper must purchase for the offer.

POP Name

Offer Type

Definition

Interstitial_CrossSell

Cross-Sell

An interstitial page that appears between the Product Detail and Shopping Cart pages that offers another product at a reduced price. When creating an offer with this POP you must use the Cross-Sell offer type.

Interstitial_UpSell

Up-Sell

An interstitial page that appears between the Product Detail and Shopping Cart pages that offers a better version of the product. If the shopper chooses the up-sell product, the original product is replaced in the shopping cart. When creating an offer with this POP you must use the Up-Sell offer type.

Pop-Up POPs

Pop-up POPs are used to show offers in a pop-up or drop-in window that is triggered when the shopper enters or exits your store. We recommend you use the "Discount" offer type when creating offers with these POPs.

POP Name

Definition

PopUp_FirstTimeVisitor_Activator

Activator for the "PopUp_FirstTimeVisitor" POP. You must create a separate offer that uses this POP before you create the offer that uses the "PopUp_FirstTimeVisitor" POP or the pop-up will not work on your store.

PopUp_FirstTimeVisitor

Pop-up that appears to first-time visitors and offers them a special discount or promotion. When a shopper clicks the offer image, the offer is applied to their session and the pop-up closes. If the shopper makes a qualifying purchase, the offer they clicked will be applied to their order.

PopUp_WaitDontLeave_Activator

Activator for the "PopUp_WaitDontLeave" POP. You must create a separate offer that uses this POP before you create the offer that uses the "PopUp_WaitDontLeave" POP or the pop-up will not work on your store.

PopUp_WaitDontLeave

Pop-up that appears when a shoppers tries to navigate away from the store when there are items in their shopping cart. When a shopper clicks the offer image, the offer is applied to their session and the pop-up closes. If the shopper makes a qualifying purchase the offer they clicked will be applied to their order

Shopping Cart POPs

Shopping Cart POPs appear as banners or other visual elements on the Shopping Cart page. You can use the Shopping Cart POPs to promote products such as CDToGo/Backup Media or Cross-Sell/Up-Sell products.

Tip: If your store offers CDToGo/Backup Media, we recommend you use the "CandyRack_ShoppingCart" POP to promote this product/service.

POP Name

Definition

Banner_ShoppingCartLocal

Banner ad that appears at the top of the shopping cart. The banner can be clickable to open a product details or category page, or initiate some other action on the store (requires Site Design work).

When creating an offer for this POP we recommend you use the Banner offer type.

CandyRack_ShoppingCart

Promotion that appears in the shopping cart as a line item. Shopper can add the item to their shopping cart by clicking an "Add" button.

When you create an offer for this POP we recommend you use the Cross-Sell offer type.

PointOfSale_CouponEntryField

Enables the coupon codes you’ve added to the offer and allows them to be used in the coupon code field that appears in the shopping cart.

When you create an offer for this POP, we recommend you use the Discount offer type (but you can use other types if appropriate).
