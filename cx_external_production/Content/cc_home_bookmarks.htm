Bookmarks

The Bookmarks feature allows you to bookmark any page in the Command Console for quick access. Bookmarks are accessible from both the Home page and from the toolbar. Click here to see an example of both areas where Bookmarks are accessed.

Using Bookmarks

To add, reorder, or delete bookmarks, click Organize Bookmarks in the Bookmarks pod on the Home page or in the Bookmark drop-down in the upper-right corner of the screen.

Adding Bookmarks

To add a bookmark in the Organize Bookmarks window, drag the name of the page you want to bookmark from the Available Links list on the left to the Bookmarks list on the right. A yellow rectangle appears to show you where the new bookmark will appear.

To delete a bookmark, click the trash icon that appears next to a bookmark name.

Pages that are greyed in the Available Links list have already been added to your Bookmarks list.
