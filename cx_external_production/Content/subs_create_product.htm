Creating a Subscription Product

Creating a new subscription product is very similar to creating any other new product. This topic give a basic overview of how to create a simple subscription product. Your subscription products may be more complicated based on other options you want to select for the product.

Tip: We have found that using a 5 day bill auto renew prior to billing time frame with a 7 day grace period provides the best renewal rate.

  1. To create a new subscription product, open the New Product wizard and start creating the product as you normally would.
  2. When you reach the fourth step of the wizard (the Product Options step) make sure you select the Subscription check box.


  3. After you have selected the Subscription option in the fourth step, continue to the sixth step (the Product Settings step) and then define the subscription options in the Subscription area.
