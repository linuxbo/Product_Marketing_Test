About Product Offers

A product offer is one that promotes specific products or provides a discount for one or more products in your store. Product offers can be created for a single product, a group of products, all products in one or more categories, or all products in your store.

Types of Product Offers

Product offers are used to create offers that discount or promote one or more products on your store.

Note: Some of the offer types listed below may not appear when you create an offer if your site has not been set up to use them. See Configuring Offer Types for more information about how to set up offer types for your site.
