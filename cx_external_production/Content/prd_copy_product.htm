Copying Products

You can create one or more copies of a product, including any variations for the product. After you copy, you can modify the copied products to create new, unique products in your catalog.

To copy a product:

Role Needed: Catalog ManagerA user role that allows the user access to the catalog and product features. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information.
  1. First, locate and open the product you want to copy. To do this, on the Catalog menu, select Products and then click Manage Products. The Products list page appears.
  2. Click on the Internal Product Name to open the product.
  3. In the taskbar, click Copy this Product. The Copy this Product window appears.
  4. In the Quantity field, enter the number of copies of the product you want to make. Then, in the Options area, select the check boxes of the attributes that you want to copy with the product and click Copy.
  5. If you made multiple copies you are taken to the Products list page when the copy is complete. If you made a single copy, you see the Edit Product page when the copy is complete. You can always tell which product(s) are copies because the words "copy of"appear before the product name.
  6. Now you can make any changes to the product settings as needed. Keep in mind that digital rights are not copied so you must set up digital rights for any copied products that require it. See Product Options for information about how to define settings for products.
  1. When you are done changing the details and attributes for the copy, click Save at the bottom of the page. When the page refreshes you are taken to the Products list. Make sure you deploy the copied product if you want it to be seen or purchased on your site. See Deploying Products for more information.
