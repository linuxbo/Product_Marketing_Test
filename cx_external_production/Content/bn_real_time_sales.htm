Business Navigator: Real Time Sales

Real Time Sales reports contain information about your current sales. The Real Time Customer Transaction report will show up-to-the-minute sales and the current status of demand orders. All other Real Time Sales reports show sales with a delay up to an hour.

Report Name Description
Real Time Customer Transactions Detailed report showing up to the second sales.
Real Time Item Offer Aggregated sales grouped at the line offer levels.
Real Time Order Offer Aggregated sales grouped at the order offer levels.
Real Time Product Level Sales Aggregated sales grouped at the product level.
Real Time Product Level Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/product level.
Real Time Program Level Sales Aggregated sales grouped at the program level.
Real Time Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day level.
