Business Navigator: Subscription Reports: Failed Payment Details

The Failed Payment Details report shows detailed information about failed attempts to renew a subscription. Most attempts to renew an auto-renew subscriptions fail because of a problem with the payment authorization.

Depending on how you have a product set up, (Undefined variable: gc_cx_variables.PlatformName3) will make one to three attempts to renew an automatic renewal subscription. When an attempt fails, the shopper is sent the "User Subscription Auto Renewal Failure event" email which prompts the shopper to manually renew or update their payment information.

Depending on the date range of a report, a subscription could appear in the report multiple times (once for each failed renewal attempt within the date range of the report).

An entry in this report does not mean that the subscription was not renewed. A subsequent attempt could have been successful or the shopper could have manually renewed.

Note: Because (Undefined variable: gc_cx_variables.PlatformName3) attempts to renew a subscription multiple times (and sends a notification to shoppers when an attempt fails to encourage them to update their billing info or renew manually) this report is not ideal when you want to analyze renewal rates or impact on revenue. To get a accurate picture of renewal rates for your subscription we recommend you use the Cancel Details report.

Fields in the Report

Field Definition/Meaning
Payment Attempt #

The number of attempts made to auto-renew a subscription. The number of attempts that are made is determined by an option in the subscription product setup.

Payment Attempt Failure Code

Code that indicates why a renewal failed. Digital River uses Paymentech codes to indicate why a renewal failed. For information about the Paymentech codes, see the following:

  • Codes for Debit Cards
  • Codes for Stored Value Cards
  • Codes for Credit Cards 
Payment Attempt Reason

Additional context information received directly from the payment providers. It varies by payment provider and the contents of this field is not defined by Digital River nor does DR guarantee the content of this field. This field is provided as is with the intent that it might provide additional context from the provider if available.
