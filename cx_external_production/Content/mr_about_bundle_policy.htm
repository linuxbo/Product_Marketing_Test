About Bundle Policy

In bundle offers the relationship between the parent and child products is called the bundle policy. When you create an offer you select how you want the parent and child products to behave in relation to each other and that in turns defines the bundle policy the offer assumes.

Note: The bundle policy is not applicable to custom bundles. See About Custom Bundles for more about custom bundle offers.

Setting Bundle Policy

You can see what policy will be used for your offer in the "Bundle Options" area of the Offer Wizard. You may notice that as you select different behavior options, the policy changes to reflect the setup. The picture below shows the bundle options with the bundle policy area near the bottom.

Explanation of Bundle Policies

This following explains the bundle policies an offer can have and shows how you can make your offer use a specific policy with the bundle options.

Loose Bundle

  • Child product quantity is not tied to the parent product quantity.
  • Parent and child products can be removed independently of each other.

When This Happens... This Happens...
Parent product added to shopping cart Child product added to cart
Quantity of parent product is changed by shopper No change to child product quantity
Parent product is deleted from shopping cart No change to child product
Quantity of child product is changed by shopper No change to parent product quantity
Child product is deleted from shipping cart No change to parent product

Semi-Tight Bundle

  • Child product quantity is not tied to the parent product quantity.
  • Removing the parent product also removes the child product.

When This Happens... This Happens...
Parent product added to shopping cart Child product added to cart
Quantity of parent product is changed by shopper Child updated so that it is not higher than parent
Parent product is deleted from shopping cart Parent product and child product are both deleted
Quantity of child product is changed by shopper N/A â€“ Child product quantity cannot be changed
Child product is deleted from shipping cart No change to parent product

Note: If a store does not have the "allow duplicate line items" setting enabled for a site, and a shopper adds a bundle with the "tight" policy to the shopping cart and then adds a bundle with the "semi-tight" policy that contains a product from the tight bundle, only the non-shared products in the semi-tight bundle will be added to the cart. For example, bundle #1 (the tight bundle) has products A and B. Bundle #2 (the semi-tight bundle) has products B and C. When the shopper attempts to add bundle #2 to the cart, only product C will be added because it already exists in the cart from bundle #1.

Tight Bundle

  • Child product quantity must match parent product quantity.
  • Removing either product (parent or child) removes both.

When This Happens... This Happens...
Parent product added to shopping cart Child product added to cart
Quantity of parent product is changed by shopper Child product quantity changed to match parent product quantity
Parent product is deleted from shopping cart Parent product and child product is deleted
Quantity of child product is changed by shopper Parent product quantity changed to match child product quantity
Child product is deleted from shipping cart Parent product and child product is deleted

Note: If a store does not have the "allow duplicate line items" setting enabled for a site, and a shopper adds a bundle with the "tight" policy to the shopping cart and then adds a bundle with the "semi-tight" policy that contains a product from the tight bundle, only the non-shared products in the semi-tight bundle will be added to the cart. For example, bundle #1 (the tight bundle) has products A and B. Bundle #2 (the semi-tight bundle) has products B and C. When the shopper attempts to add bundle #2 to the cart, only product C will be added because it already exists in the cart from bundle #1.

Tight Bundle - Independent Child Quantity

  • Child product quantity is not tied to parent product quantity.
  • Removing either product (parent or child) removes both.

When This Happens... This Happens...
Parent product added to shopping cart Child product added to cart
Quantity of parent product is changed by shopper No change to child product
Parent product is deleted from shopping cart Parent product and child product are deleted
Quantity of child product is changed by shopper No change to parent product
Child product is deleted from shipping cart Parent product and child product are deleted
