Searching and Viewing Entitlements

An entitlement is created when a shopper purchases or activates a SoftwarePassport-wrapped product. The entitlement contains order and shopper information, such as the serial number for the product and the name of the shopper who purchased the product. Each time the serial number is reissued to the shopper, the entitlement information is updated.

You can search serial numbers of SoftwarePassport-wrapped products and view the serial number status and associated entitlements.

To search and view entitlements:

  1. On the Customer Service menu, select SoftwarePassport, and click Search Entitlements. The Search Serial Number for Entitlement page appears.
  2. In the Product ID field, enter the product ID with the serial number(s) you want to view entitlements for.
  3. In the Serial Number field, enter the serial number with the entitlements you want to view.
  4. Click Search. The search is performed and the results appear on the Serial Number List page.
  5. Click View Entitlements for the serial number you want to view. The View Entitlements page on the SoftwarePassport activation server appears. On this page you can see the entitlement information. This page shows information about the entitlement such as platform, license type, license locale, license product, license state, purchase type, remaining activations, remaining reissues, parent entitlement, and license expiration date. At the bottom of the page you can see the History Log for the entitlement. The History Log shows information about action on the entitlement, such as reissues and activations.
  6. While viewing the entitlement, you can do the following:
    • To view the dispenser settings, click View Dispenser Settings.
    • To view the parent entitlement, click the ID link next to the Parent Entitlement label.
    • To increase the number of activations or resissues, enter a number in the appropriate field and click Increase.
    • To search the entitlements again, click Search Entitlements.
    • To deactivate the entitlement, click Deactivate Entitlement.
