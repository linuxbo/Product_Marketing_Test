About Supported Locales

When you create an offer, you can choose which locales you want the offer to support, and which locale you want to be the default for the offer. Only those locales that you select for the offer will be able to see and redeem the offer on your store.

You can also localize the offer display content so that the sales pitch, offer text, and so on appears in the language for a specific locale. This is called localizing the offer content, or localizing the offer. For more information about localizing your offers, see About Localizing Offers.
