Tax Settings

The Tax settings are used to classify the product into a category for tax purposes. You must assign a Tax Group and Tax Type to a product before you can deploy that product to your store. If you are unsure which category you should assign to your products, consult a tax professional as they can help you determine which code should be used for your products. Digital River cannot instruct you on the tax settings you should use.

How Taxes are Calculated on Your Store

When a product is purchased on your store the taxes are automatically calculated based on a variety of factors. This includes the tax group and tax type, as well as ship-from address (product source), ship-to address (product destination), and product weight.

We use a highly respected and powerful system to calculate tax on orders. By creating tax groups and tax types for you to classify your product we have made it easier for our tax system to accurately calculate tax no matter what the product is or where in the world the order is placed.

Setting Default Tax Group and Tax Type

You can set which group and type is selected by default using the Product Template (see About the Product Template for more information). If you need a tax group or type that is not in the list, contact your Store Operations team.

Tax Attributes

Attribute Definition
Tax Group Grouping used to determine the specific tax types you can select for the product.
Tax Type Specific tax category for the product used to calculate tax.
