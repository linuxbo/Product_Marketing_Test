Viewing the Email Marketing Dashboard

The Email Marketing dashboard shows information on your BlueHornet email marketing campaigns and promotions. If you do not have a BlueHornet account you will not see any data in the Email Marketing dashboard.

Tip: The Email Marketing dashboard uses many of the same elements as many of the other Marketing Dashboards in the Command Console. To learn more about using and interacting with the Marketing Dashboards, see About Marketing Dashboards.

  1. On the Reports menu, select Marketing Dashboard, and click Email Marketing. The Email Marketing dashboard page appears. See example.

Choosing the Data to View

  1. In the Customize View area at the top, from the Sales Type list, select the type of sales you want to see on the dashboard:
    • Demand – Orders that have been placed but not fulfilled (downloaded or shipped). Includes orders placed with delayed payment methods. Does not include returns or cancellations.
    • Fulfilled – Orders that have been placed and fulfilled (downloaded or shipped). This includes orders placed with delayed payment methods. This does not include returns or cancellations.
  2. From the Time Frame list, select the time frame for which you want to view data in the dashboard. For information on how time frames are calculated, see Time Frame.
  3. From the Currency list, select the currency you want to use to view the data in the dashboard. For more information about currency in dashboards, see Currency.
  4. Click Apply Settings. The dashboard refreshes to show what you have defined.

Exporting the Dashboard Data

  1. If you want to export the Dashboard data to a Microsoft® Excel® spreadsheet, click Export in the upper-right corner of the page. The exported spreadsheet will contain a tab of data for the Dashboard.

Viewing Email Performance

The Email Performance area shows more detail about message activity in the time frame you have chosen for the dashboard.

Note: Because the dashboard shows data just for the time period you have selected, you may see KPIs that seem incorrect. For example, you could see "0" for Emails Sent but also see a positive value in Bounces & Invalids. This would seem incorrect because you must send a message to have a bounce or an invalid. However, the bounces or invalids you see in the dashboard could be from a message that was sent prior to the time period shown in the dashboard.

KPI Definition and Calculation
Emails Sent Total number of messages sent.
Bounces & Invalids Total number of messages that bounced or were invalid.
Emails Delivered Total number of messages delivered.
Opens

Total number of messages opened.

Calculation: Total HTML Opens + Total Unique Messages Clicked
Open Rate

Percentage of delivered messages that were opened.

Total Unique Opened/Total Delivered * 100
Clicks Total unique subscribers who clicked a link in a message.
Email Sales

Total of all orders that have been tracked.

Demand: Orders that have been placed but not fulfilled (downloaded or shipped). Includes orders placed with delayed payment methods. Does not include returns or cancellations.

Fulfilled: Orders that have been placed and fulfilled (downloaded or shipped). This includes orders placed with delayed payment methods. This does not include returns or cancellations.
Units Total of items ordered.
Orders

Total orders that tracked back to a specific message.

Note: Depending on the tracking method some of your sales may not track back to a specific message. Some tracking methods can only be tracked back to the BlueHornet account/client and are not included in Total Orders.
Response Rate Rate of orders to the rate of messages delivered.
Order-to-click Rate Rate of unique orders to unique clicks.
Sales/Email Average sales per message sent.
Average Order Value Average total value of all orders.
