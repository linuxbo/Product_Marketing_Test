Viewing Refund Details

The Refund Details page lists specific details about refund issued for a specific order. From this page you can take certain actions on the refund and extend the due date for the return

  • To view the refund instructions, click View Instructions.
  • Next to the Action label, several different buttons may appear, such as Refund Without Return or Acknowledge ELOD.
Click here to see a sample of a button by the Action label.

  • To extend the due date for the return, in the Extend field, enter the number of days to extend and the reason for the extension in the Extend Comments field. Then, click Extend Due Date at the bottom of the page.

  • To view the order summary, click Order Details at the bottom of the page. The Order Summary page appears.
  • To go to the Refund page where you can initiate a refund, click Refund at the bottom of the page.

The tables below outline the fields and information that appears for the refund on the page. The first table outlines the refund details, while the second outlines specific product information in the refund.

Click here to see a sample of the refund information and area summarized below.

Label Description
Refund Number The system-assigned refund number for this order.
Instructions Click View Instructions to go to the Refund Instructionspage for a quick summary of any actions the user needs to take for the refund.
Order Number The identification number automatically assigned to the order.
Generation Date The date and time the refund was requested.
Generated By The login ID of the person who issued the refund.
Type Refund type, whether Order Level or Product Level refund.
Policy Lists return policy for the products.
Status The current status of the refund.
Reason Shows the reason for the refund.
Comments Any applicable comments added to this refund.
Date Refunded The date of the refund.
Total Refunded The total amount refunded at this time.
Outstanding The amount outstanding on the refund request.
Total Requested The total amount requested for the refund.
Overrides Lists site policies overridden for the refund request.

Specific information about the products in the order being refunded is shown near the bottom, itemized by product.

Label Description
Product Name The name of the product in the refund.
Status The current status of the refund for the product.
Due Date The due date for the refund of the product.
Extend Used to enter a number of days to extend the due date.
Extend Comments Used to enter comments about a due date extension.

Qty Returned The actual number of the product returned for the refund.
