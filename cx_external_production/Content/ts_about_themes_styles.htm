Themes and Styles

The themes and styles features are used to control the look and feel of pages on your store. A style defines the colors, fonts, buttons, images, style sheet, and some HTML code used by your store. Styles are then grouped together into themes that you apply to some or all of the pages used on your store. If your store supports multiple locales you can choose which locale uses a specific style in a theme.

Note: Themes and styles are not available for stores that use the Headwater template. This means that if your store uses the Headwater template, you will not see the themes and styles options in the Store Builder menu as these Help topics indicate. To find out what template your store uses, contact your CMM.
