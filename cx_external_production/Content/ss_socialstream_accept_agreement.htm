Accepting the SocialStream Service Agreement

A site representative will need to accept the Digital River SocialStream Agreement in order to use the SocialStream feature. The representative can be assigned either the SocialStream ManagerA user role that provides access to SocialStream. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information. role or the Legal Agreement ManagerA user role that allows the user to view and approve legal agreements. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information. role.

Note to Internal Users: Digital River internal users can accept the SocialStream Service Agreement on behalf on clients.

Accepting the SocialStream Service Agreement as SocialStream Manager

If you are assigned the SocialStream Manager role, you can accept the agreement during the SocialStream setup process.

Role Needed: SocialStream ManagerA user role that provides access to SocialStream. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information.
  1. On the Marketing menu, select SocialStream, and click Dashboard. The Get Started with SocialStream page appears.
  2. In the Twitter or Facebook area, click Add Twitter Account or Add Facebook Account depending on the social networking account you want to set up. If you have not already accepted the Digital River SocialStream Agreement, a new page appears indicating that you must accept the agreement before proceeding.
  3. Click Yes to continue to the SocialStream Service Agreement.
  4. If, after reading the SocialStream Service Agreement you choose to accept it, select the appropriate option at the bottom of the page, enter your name, title, and email address in the appropriate fields, and click Next. The page refreshes and a message appears indicating that you have accepted the agreement and can begin using SocialStream on your store.
  5. To begin using SocialStream, on the Marketing menu, select SocialStream, and click SocialStream Dashboard. Or, while still viewing the agreement, click the get started now link.

Accepting the SocialStream Service Agreement as Legal Agreement Manager

If you are assigned the Legal Agreement Manager role, you can accept the agreement outside of the SocialStream setup process.

Role Needed: Legal Agreement ManagerA user role that allows the user to view and approve legal agreements. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information.
  1. On the Administration menu, select Company and click Legal Agreements.
  2. From the list of legal agreements, select the SocialStream Service Agreement.
  3. If, after reading the SocialStream Service Agreement you choose to accept it, select the appropriate option at the bottom of the page, enter your name, title, and email address in the appropriate fields, and click Next. The page refreshes and a message appears indicating that you have accepted the agreement and can begin using SocialStream on your store.
  4. To begin using SocialStream, on the Marketing menu, select SocialStream, and click SocialStream Dashboard. Or, while still viewing the agreement, click the get started now link.
