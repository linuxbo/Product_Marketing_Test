About Localization

Localization is a term used to refer to the process of customizing your store for different nationalities, cultures, languages, and currencies. When you localize, you are essentially creating a shopping experience tailored to shoppers in other countries, also called locales. There are many ways you can localize your store and many options you can use to control how your store looks and behaves to shoppers in other locales.

Localization can be anything from changing which language or currency is used, to adapting your store to account for cultural or regional differences in shopper expectations and behavior, to modifying the date and time formats to reflect regional use. For example, you can localize your products so that shoppers see product information in their native language and currency. You can also localize themes and styles to create a look and feel that is specific for a locale or region.
