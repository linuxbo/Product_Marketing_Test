Business Navigator: Subscription Reports: Cancel Details

The Cancel Details report shows detailed information about subscriptions that have an Expiration Date and were canceled within the time frame defined for the report. Canceled subscriptions with an Expiration Date outside the time frame for the report will not appear.

Determining Why a Subscription Was Canceled

Subscriptions are canceled for one of the following reasons:

  • The shopper did not do a manual renew of their subscription.
  • The shopper canceled their subscription using the self-service feature, or called Customer Service to cancel their subscription.
  • Payment for the auto-renew subscription could not be authorized. Digital River makes several attempts to authorize payment for automatic-renewal subscriptions. Reports may show only

Figuring out why a subscription was canceled can be done by looking at "Cancellation Date" and "Subscription Expiration Date" in the report. You will also need to know the Grace Period set for the product/subscription.

  • If Cancellation Date is less than or equal to (Subscription Expiration Date + Grace Period) it means the shopper took action to cancel the subscription. This could mean they called Customer Service or they canceled the subscription themselves using the subscription self-service tools.
  • If Cancellation Date is greater than (Subscription Expiration Date + Grace Period) it means the subscription was canceled because the shopper did not renew the subscription (if the subscription is a manual renewal) or the payment didn't authorize (if the subscription is an auto-renewal).

Tip: We recommend adding a column to the report which displays "Shopper Canceled" or "Expired" based on the criteria above. This will make it easy to identify why a subscription was canceled.

Subscriptions with a Cancellation Reason

The Cancellation Reason will only appear if the shopper called Customer Service to return the subscription. Subscriptions that are canceled for other reasons or using other methods will not have a cancellation reason in this report.
