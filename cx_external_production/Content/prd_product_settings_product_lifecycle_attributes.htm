Product Lifecycle

The Product Lifecycle setting is used to define when you want your product to be available for sale on your store. You choose a start and end date for the product, deploy it, and the system will hide and show the product based on the dates you have selected.

Product Lifecycle Attributes

Attribute Definition
Start Date

The day and time on which you want the product to appear on your store. This assumes that the "Display Product" and "Purchasable" options are selected, and that the product has been deployed.

Note: When you set a start date in the lifecycle settings you must deploy the product in order for that product to be available on your store when the start date is reached. Products that reach their start date and are not deployed will not be seen on your store.
End Date The day and time on which you want the product to be automatically hidden on your store.

End of Life Notification
Notification

Used to indicate whether you want an email notification sent when the product is about to end.  
No Notification – No email notification will be sent informing you when the product.
Email Notification – Notification(s) will be sent using the time frames you define.

Time Frame 1 The first time frame for which you want to receive an email notification when the product lifecycle is about to end.
Time Frame 2 The second time frame for which you want to receive an email notification when the product lifecycle is about to end.
Time Frame 3 The third time frame for which you want to receive an email notification when the product lifecycle is about to end.
