Choosing the Offer Trigger

The offer trigger determines how the offer is activated or redeemed by shoppers. Depending on the trigger, shoppers may have to click a link or enter a code during checkout to redeem an offer.

The offer trigger is selected in the first step (the "Type" step) of the offer wizard. Once you create and deploy an offer you cannot change the offer trigger.

Always Triggered

  • Shoppers have to click a link to redeem the offer.
  • Can be limited by how many times an individual shopper can redeem the offer.

Promotional URL

  • Shoppers have to enter a coupon code in the Shopping Cart to redeem the offer.
  • Can be limited by how many times an individual shopper can redeem the offer.

Coupon Code
