Subscription Auto Renew

The Auto Renew option is used to indicate whether you want the subscription to automatically renew (and the shopper billed) when the subscription expires.

  • If you do not select this option, the subscription will expire unless the shopper renews the subscription themselves using the self-service options.
  • If you select this option, Digital River will attempt to renew the subscription based upon what you select for the Bill Auto Renew Prior to Expiration option.

To learn more about how subscriptions renew, see Subscription Renewals.
