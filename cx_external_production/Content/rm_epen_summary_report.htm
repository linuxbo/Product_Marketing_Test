Summary Report

The Summary report contains the information listed below. Some of this data may not be calculated for the store because of the store set up and configuration.

Field

Definition

Region Code

Region where the sale took place.

Currency

The currency that will be used to pay client, per the contract.

Gross Sales

Total amount of sales, before discounts. May include VAT.

Less Discounts

Total amount of discounts.

Net Sales

Total of sales, after discounts, including tax.

Less Refunds

Total amount of refunds and returns.

Net Price

Gross Price less Discounts. The final price the customer paid.

Add Remit Shipping

The amount paid to a third-party fulfillment agency for shipping costs they occurred, but that were paid to Digital River.

Less Shipping Discount

Total shipping discounts applied that are funded by the client.

Less Trans Fee

Total transaction fees applied (for example, Free Order Fee, Large File Download Fee)

Less Non Trans Fee

Total non-transaction fees applied (Hosting Fee, Setup Fees, and so on).

Less Cost of Sales

Digital River’s margin amount (Net Price less Net Cost).

Total Amount Due

Amount due to client (Net Price, less fees and Digital River’s margin).

Shipping VAT

VAT based on shipping costs. This is not used when calculating the amount due to client.

Product VAT

VAT based on product costs. This is not used when calculating the amount due to client.
