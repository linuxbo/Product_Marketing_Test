About Deployment Warnings and Errors

When working with a product you may see messages at the top of the page about errors or missing information for the product. These messages are called "deployment warnings" and "deployment errors" and they help you determine if your product is set up correctly and ready to be deployed on your store.

Products with errors cannot be deployed and the button to deploy a product will be disabled in the UI until you fix all the errors for a product. Products with warnings can still be deployed but we recommend that you fix all warnings before deploying because the product may not appear or function as expected when it has a warning. For more about what happens when you deploy a product, About Deploying Products.

In addition to seeing the errors or warnings for a product when you view it, you can also see errors and warnings on the Undeployed Changes tab on the Deploy Products page.

Deployment Warnings

A deployment warning is just as the name implies: a warning you should be know about before you deploy the product. Deployment warnings appear when something about the product setup is not quite right but the issue is not so severe that it affects a shopper’s ability to purchase the product on your store.

We recommend you fix all deployment warnings before you deploy a product to your store. You can still deploy a product that has warnings but it might cause unexpected behavior or problems with the sale of that product. If you see deployment warnings that you are unsure how to fix, please contact your Store Operations team.

Deployment Errors

A deployment error indicates a major setup issue with the product that will likely affect the sale and fulfillment of the product on your store. Products with deployment errors cannot be deployed until all errors are fixed. This is to prevent you from accidentally deploying a product that will essentially fail to be sold correctly on your store.

Viewing Deployment Errors and Deployment Warnings

If you are viewing the Undeployed Changes tab on the Deploy Products page, you may see two different small icons: red octagons for errors, yellow triangles for warnings. These icons will appear next to products that have deployment warnings or errors. To view the details of the warning or error, click the plus sign next to the icon.
