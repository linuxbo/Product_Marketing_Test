EDS/CDToGo

The EDS/CDToGo report contains the information listed below. Some of this data may not be calculated for the store because of the store set up and configuration.

Field

Definition

Region Code

Region where the sale took place.

Currency

Currency of the transaction.

Gross Sales

Total sales amount excluding tax, shipping, refunds, and discounts.

Less Discounts

Total amount of discounts applied.

Net Sales

Gross Sales less Discounts.

Less Shipping

Total of shipping discounts that are funded by the client.

Less Refunds

Total amount of refunds issued.

Net Price

Total of Net Sales less Tax, Shipping, and Refunds.

Less Trans Fees

Total transaction fees (Free Order Fee, Large File Download Fee, etc.).

Less Non Trans Fees

Total of non-transaction fees (Hosting Fee, Setup Fees, etc.).

Less Cost of Sales

Digital River’s margin amount.

Total Amount Due

Net Price less Trans Fees, Non-Trans Fees, and Cost of Sales (amount of payment due).
