About Fulfillment Acknowledgement Errors

When you (a fulfiller) marks an item with an error on the Fulfillment Acknowledgement page, a notification is sent to Digital River Store Operations notifying them of the error so that they can investigate and take corrective action.

The picture below shows an order where one item has been marked with a ShipTo Address error. Because this item has been marked with an error, the Package Quantity field is greyed out (unavailable) for that line item. The field will remain unavailable until the error is fixed.
