Digital River Suite: Logging On to an Application

Once you have added an application, you can log on to that application with one click.

Opening the Digital River Suite Ribbon

  1. In the upper-center of the screen, click the Digital River Suite link. The Digital River Suite ribbon opens.

Logging On to an Application

  1. Click Log On under the application you want to access. The systems attempts to log on you on to the Digital River Suite application.

    If the login fails, it may be because your password for the system has changed. See Managing Applications to learn how to change your password for the application.
