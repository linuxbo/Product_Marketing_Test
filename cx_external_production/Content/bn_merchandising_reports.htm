Business Navigator: Merchandising Reports

Merchandising reports offer glimpses into various marketing activities and can help answer the question "how are people getting to my store."

Report Name Description
Order Line Offers Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/line offer levels.
Order Line Offer Summary Aggregated sales grouped at the day level.
Order Offer Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/order offer levels.
Order Offer Summary Aggregated sales grouped at the day level.
Program Activity By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/program level.
Program Activity Summary Aggregated sales grouped at the program level.
Program Offer Summary Aggregated sales grouped at the program/line offer level.
Program Sales By Day Aggregated sales grouped at the day/program level.
Program Sales By Order Line Offer Aggregated sales grouped at the program/line offer level.
Program Sales By Product Aggregated sales grouped at the program/product level.
Program Summary Aggregated sales grouped at the program level.
