Viewing Product Performance

Role Needed: Catalog ManagerA user role that allows the user access to the catalog and product features. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information. and Revenue ScorecardA user role that provides access to the Revenue Scorecard Dashboard. Search this file for the "user role definitions" topic for more information.

When you view the details of a product you can see the demand sales for that product. This allows you to see how your products are performing and determine how to leverage the success of your best performers to build more sales.

The information you see when viewing an product is the same as what you see when viewing the Revenue Scorecard dashboard. If you want to view more information about how an offer is performing, use the Revenue Scorecard dashboard.

To see how a product is performing, first open the product you want to analyze. There are two ways to locate and open an product:

  • Use Quick Search to find a product by name or ID.

  • Navigate to the Products list page (Catalog > Products > Manage Products) and click on the product you want to view.

Once you have a product open, on the Summary tab, you will see a Performance area.

  • Click Settings to define the Time Frame and Currency you want to see.
  • Click Settings to define the Time Frame and Currency you want to see.

If the product is an individual product, you will see a graph showing the demand sales for the product.

If the product is a multiple product with variations you will see one of the variations plotted in the graph. You can then select up to three (3) additional variations from the list below the graph that you want to also view.

To learn more about how demand sales are calculated, see Viewing the Revenue Scorecard Dashboard.
