About the Home Page

The first thing you see when you log on to the Command Console is the Home page. The Home page helps you stay informed about events happening on your store and at Digital River. It also keeps you informed about changes Digital River has made in the recent release and helps you learn more about optimizing your Command Console experience.

The following list outlines the Home page elements:

  • Viewing Site Trends (Legacy) – Shows information about traffic and orders for the currently selected site.
  • News & Notes – Contains articles about how to optimize your store and use Command Console features and information about the latest release.
  • Important Notices – Appears only when system maintenance has been planned and informs you when maintenance will occur and what will be affected.
  • Events – Shows a list of events occurring on your store.
  • Recent Items – Shows those items you recently created, edited, or viewed.
  • Bookmarks – Shows the pages you have added as bookmarks.
  • Important Dates – Shows a list of holidays and office closures for Digital River offices.
  • Help Center – Contains links to Help Central topics.

Customizing the Home Page Pods

You can customize the Home page specifically for your needs. You can collapse those pods that you do not use frequently and rearrange the pods so that those pods you use most often are at the top of the Home page.

To collapse and expand Home page pods:

  • Click the + or - icon near the pod title. If a pod is collapsed, it won't load when the Home page is loaded. This can be especially helpful with Site Trends, as not everyone needs to see that data each time the Home page loads and collapsing the pod will mean the Home page will load faster.

To rearrange the Home page pods:

  • Click on the pod title and drag the pod to the new position on the Home page. The Home page has two columns of pods: the larger column of pods along the left and the smaller column of pods along the right. Pods can only be rearranged within their current column.
