About Estimates

An estimate is an approximate total cost for an order, including any taxes, fees, and shipping costs. Buyers can request an estimate through the shopping cart. After the estimate request is sent, an email is sent to the buyer with a link that they can click to access the estimate and complete their order.

Note: The estimates feature is only available for sites that use the Delta template. You can see which template your site uses when you configure your site settings.

Enabling Estimates

If you want buyers to be able to request an estimate on your store, you have to enable the request estimates feature in site settings. Once enabled, the "Request Estimate" button will appear in the shopping cart and buyers will be able to request an estimate.

Estimate Settings

When you enable estimates there are several settings that also must be configured:

  • How long a buyer can place an order for an estimate (how long estimates last).
  • How long after an estimate is created the buyer is sent a reminder email about the estimate (only one reminder email is sent).
  • Whether an estimate can be used multiple times (buyers can place multiple orders for a single estimate).
  • Whether the buyer can opt to have a customer service representative contact them about their estimate.

Creating/Requesting Estimates

A buyer (or client representative shopping on behalf of a shopper) can request an estimate by clicking the "Request Estimate" button in the shopping cart.

To submit an estimate request, buyers will have to provide a name and up to three email addresses where they want the estimate sent once it is created (one of those email addresses should be the buyer's email address). Other information such as company name and phone number can also be entered but are not required to complete the estimate request. If the buyer wants the estimate to include taxes, fees, and shipping costs they will have to provide a shipping and billing address.

Once the buyer completes and submits their request, the estimate is created and the estimate email is sent to the email addresses entered for the estimate.

Viewing Existing Estimates

When an estimate is created, an email notification is sent to the email address(es) that were provided when the estimate was requested. The email contains a copy of the estimate with a link to view the estimate and/or complete the order.

When a buyer is viewing an estimate they are notified if the product price, taxes, fees, shopping costs, or product availability has changed since the estimate was created. Pricing and product availability are not guaranteed with estimates. If a buyer needs an exact price for their products, they should request a quote.

If a buyer attempts to view an estimate after the time frame that has been set up for estimates to be valid, the buyer is notified that the estimate is no longer available.

Editing Estimates

When a buyer is viewing an estimate they can change the quantity and products in the cart, which changes the estimate. Estimates will be recalculated based on the new cart contents. Estimates are always updated when the cart contents or the payment information changes.

Mini Cart Widget and Estimates

If a store is using the mini-cart widget, the mini-cart will also show an estimate when a buyer is viewing their estimate for the store.
