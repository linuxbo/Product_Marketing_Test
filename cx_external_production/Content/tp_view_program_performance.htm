Viewing Tracking Program Performance

When you view the details of a tracking program you can see the demand sales, close rate, and unique visitors for that tracking program. This allows you to edit or retire tracking programs that are not performing as well as you hoped. This can also help you determine which tracking programs are your best performers so you can replicate that success in other promotions.

The information you see when viewing a tracking program is the same as what you see when viewing the Merchandising Dashboard. If you want to view more information about how a tracking program is performing, use the Merchandising Dashboard.

To see how a tracking program is performing, first open the offer you want to analyze. There are two ways to locate and open an tracking program:

  • Use Quick Search to find a tracking program by name or ID.

  • Navigate to the Tracking Programs list page (Marketing > Offers and Programs > Manage Tracking Programs) and click on the tracking program you want to view.

Once you have a tracking program open you will see a Performance area showing the total demand sales resulting from the tracking program, the close rate for orders placed for the tracking program, and the number of unique visitors that clicked a tracking program link. To learn more about how these are calculated, see Viewing the Merchandising Dashboard.

  • Click Settings to define the Time Frame and Currency you want to view.
  • Click Merchandising Dashboard to see more information about an offer on the Merchandising Dashboard.
