Setting Up the Supported Locales

In the wizard, the Behavior step is where you indicate if you want to localize the offer for other locales. Because offer content appears in the POP for the offer, if you do not select a POP for an offer you will not be able to enter localized offer content (offer images, descriptions, and so on). Also, if you do not enter localized content for a supported locale, the content from the default locale content will be used.

The following image shows the Behavior step and the areas that are used to set up the offer for localization. Depending on the type of offer and trigger you chose during the first step of the wizard, the fields and options you see in the Behavior step may not match the page example.
