About the Product Editor

The Product Editor is what you use to edit a product that has already been created. The Product Editor was developed so you could quickly move between the many options and settings for a product and make changes to only those settings you want, quickly and easily.

The product editor consists of three tabs: Summary, Details, and Catalogs & Categories.

When you first open the editor, you will see some information about the product above the three tabs. This information tells you the ID and name of the product, the product status, and when the product was last edited and by whom. You can also see the version of the product you are currently viewing.
